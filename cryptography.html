Cryptography and Steganography

Hello friends!

So guys, today I am going to tell you what is cryptography and steganography?

1. Cryptography

This is the process of transforming readable data into unreadable form. As an example - You have saved your password in one file and if you will do cryptography process of that file then password will be converted into hash format means anyone can't read your password.

2. Steganography

Steganography means to hide anything behind an image. As an example, hiding virus in image, hiding files and folders behind an image, binding payloads in image and etc. etc.
